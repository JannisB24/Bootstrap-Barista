Our Story

We have come a long way to introduce BootstrapBarista Cafe to the world. Grown up in a busy neighbourhood in Frankfurt, I always wondered why the people would not stop hassling around. I wanted them to make a stop, to let them breathe for a second. Thats when the idea of a Coffeeshop came to my mind. With my best friend Carlos I made this dream come true. We opened in 2019 and since then help people to find short moments of peace in a turbulent city.
